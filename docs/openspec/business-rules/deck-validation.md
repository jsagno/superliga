# Deck Validation Rules

This document defines deck and card usage rules translated from the official Spanish regulation.

## General Card Restrictions

- The organizers may restrict or ban specific cards per competition.
- Using unauthorized cards results in:
	- Match annulment.
	- Possible disciplinary sanctions.

## Extreme Cup (Extreme System)

Extreme Cup is a special competition with deck construction restrictions and strategic roles.

### Participation

- Each team must designate at least one player to participate in the Extreme Cup.
- The team captain exclusively selects the participant.

### Extremer Role

- The Extremer is the primary player for Extreme Cup.
- Organizers define a fixed pool of 24 authorized cards.
- The Extremer must use only cards from this pool.
- Any deck combination is allowed as long as all cards are from the pool.

### Risky Role

- The Risky is a special role for daily Team League duels under Extreme rules.
- The Risky must use:
	- At least 8 Extreme cards when using one deck.
	- At least 16 Extreme cards when using two decks.
- These cards must come from the same pool of 24 authorized cards.
